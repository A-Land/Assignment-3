Name of the Instatution and Program

Game-Development at Algonquin College

Entrance requirements

The requirments for the Program include:

  • Grade 12 English Credit
  • Grade 12 Math Credit (MAP4C or equivalent)
  • Grade 12 Math (MCT4C is highly recommended)
  • A credit in Physics is recommended
These are the courses that a student needs to take before entering the program.

Description of First year courses

A student in the Game-Development program would be taking these course in the first year:

  • Design Thinking
  • Comnunication I
  • Digital Imaging I
  • Game Design Foundations
  • Programming Fundamentals I
  • Technical Mathematics for Game Development
These are the courses that a student would take when first entering the program.

Other requrments

When a student enters the program they need a

  • Ontario Secondary School Diploma (OSSD)
  • Academic and Career Entrance (ACE) certificate
  • General Educational Development (GED) certificate
  • For mature students 19 years or older that do not have a high school diploma, may be eligible. They must contact the college
These are some things that are needed to enter the Program

Length of the program

The program takes between 3 years to complete.
